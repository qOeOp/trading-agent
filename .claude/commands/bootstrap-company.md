请按以下顺序工作：

1. 读取 [../../docs/workflows/company-bootstrap-loop.md](../../docs/workflows/company-bootstrap-loop.md)
2. 读取 [../../docs/organization/org-chart.md](../../docs/organization/org-chart.md)
3. 读取 [../../docs/organization/decision-rights.md](../../docs/organization/decision-rights.md)
4. 读取 [../../docs/templates/department-bootstrap-brief.md](../../docs/templates/department-bootstrap-brief.md)
5. 判断当前公司处于 bootstrap 哪个 phase
6. 列出：
   - 已初始化的治理层
   - 未初始化的部门层
   - 建议 active 的首批部门
   - 建议 dormant 的部门
7. 生成一份 bootstrap brief，并指出下一步应该是：
   - internal brainstorming
   - internal requirements
   - acceptance review
   - governance improvement
