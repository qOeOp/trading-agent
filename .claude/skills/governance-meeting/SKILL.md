准备 Founder governance meeting。

步骤：

1. 阅读 [../../../docs/workflows/founder-governance-meeting-loop.md](../../../docs/workflows/founder-governance-meeting-loop.md)。
2. 阅读 [../../../docs/workflows/volatile-research-default.md](../../../docs/workflows/volatile-research-default.md)。
3. 收集：
   - department daily reports
   - company daily digest
   - process audit
   - retro / postmortem
4. 涉及工具链、社区 best practice 或外部能力判断时，先走 internal `research dispatch`，再补外部来源或明确标记 `needs freshness check`。
5. 用 [../../../docs/templates/governance-meeting-brief.md](../../../docs/templates/governance-meeting-brief.md) 形成会议包。
6. 会议结束后，用 [../../../docs/templates/meeting-minutes.md](../../../docs/templates/meeting-minutes.md) 输出会议纪要。
